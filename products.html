Breads



Our loaves are hand-crafted daily, in a variety of flavours, shapes,
and sizes by our skilled bread bakers. Bakeology breads are pre-fermented, kneaded,
shaped by hand, let to rise, and then baked in a European stone hearth oven;
a process that takes up to 18 hours from beginning to end. A combination of
artistry and craftsmanship, our breads are made without taking shortcuts, so
they will be the most flavourful, most healthy, and most beautiful loaves possible.

Pastries



Bakeology pastries are broad in variety and technique. Our pastry menu
includes traditional European style viennoiserie of croissant,
brioche, scones and well as American classics like cookies, muffins, cinnamon rolls
and sticky buns. We bake our delicious pastries
fresh every day starting at 4AM!

Community



At Bakeology, community is a guiding principle. We consider our community in
all that we do.Donation: Any pastries that are not purchased by the end of
the day, get donated to the Community Kitchen.Local Farmers: Feed the pigs!
To meet one of our goals of producing as little waste as possible, Bakeology
donates its outdated breads weekly to several local farmers.Composting &
Recycling: Bakeology has maintained an active composting program since
2002, and now generates over 600kgs of compostable food waste every month,
and diverts over 7,500 kgs of waste from our local landfill each year.
